GigaSudoku Instructions

Welcome to GigaSudoku! This web game offers large-scale Sudoku grids designed for hardcore players who find normal 9x9 grids too simple.

Rules: Fill the board so that every row, column, and sub-block (e.g. 5x5 sub-blocks for 25x25) contains every symbol exactly once.

Controls:

  • Select a cell: Click or tap any cell on the board.
  • Zoom & Pan: Scroll your mouse wheel or pinch to zoom. Click and drag the board to pan around. Highly recommended for 25x25 and 36x36!
  • Keyboard: Use Arrow keys to navigate. Type numbers or letters to input. Press N to toggle Notes. Press Backspace / Delete to clear.
  • Virtual Pad: Click on buttons in the virtual pad to input symbols. The count badge shows how many times that symbol has been placed.
